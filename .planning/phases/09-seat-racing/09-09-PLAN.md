### Test 2: Create Session Wizard (SEAT-01, SEAT-02, SEAT-03, SEAT-04, SEAT-05)
1. Click "New Session" button
2. **Step 1 (Metadata):**
   - Enter date (today)
   - Select boat class (e.g., "8+")
   - Select conditions (e.g., "calm")
   - Enter location (e.g., "Charles River")
   - Click "Next"
3. **Step 2 (Pieces):**
   - Click "+ Add Piece"
   - Verify Piece #1 appears with Boat A and Boat B
   - Enter finish time for Boat A (e.g., "1:32.5")
   - Enter finish time for Boat B (e.g., "1:33.1")
   - Add another piece with different times
   - Click "Next"
4. **Step 3 (Assignments):**
   - For each piece/boat, assign athletes to seats
   - Verify athletes matching side preference are highlighted
   - Verify assigned athletes are dimmed in other dropdowns
   - Click "Next"
5. **Step 4 (Review):**
   - Verify all data is displayed correctly
   - Verify "Edit" links navigate back to appropriate steps
   - Click "Create Session"
6. Verify session is created and detail panel opens
